The method

The Method

Read. Verify. Draft. In that order, always.

I.

Read the record

The office action is fetched from the USPTO and parsed into its grounds of rejection, claim by claim, reference by reference. Structure first, so every later step can be checked.

Grounds parsed§101 · 102 · 103 · 112
II.

Verify every citation

Each cited patent and publication is checked against USPTO records; each characterization is tested against the passage it cites. What cannot be confirmed is flagged before you ever see it.

Taken on the model’s wordnothing
III.

Draft for review

Arguments arrive grounded, with inline sources you can open in one click. Edit freely, then export a response to Word in filing form. You remain counsel of record; Atticus remains the clerk.
